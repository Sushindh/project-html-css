About PECCON

The quest for greener planet is one of humanity's primary concerns at present, as the environment degradation affects the planet through poor air and water qualities, heat, extreme weather conditions, and low food security. If this continues, by 2050, the existing problems will get aggravated and human will be at greater risk. To mitigate the level of environmental degradation and to create a greener future for our planet, both sustainable energy and technological innovation are to be focused parallelly. Such innovations can contribute to sustainable growth by using natural resources efficiently.

Hence the society has to be educated about the importance of sustainable future through scientific conferences by implementation of a new process, product or service, that adds market value and creates a positive impact on the environment and society.

The PECCON 2025 will be constituting a way for professionals to exchange their relevant findings and experiences thereby promoting research and development in technologies that will enhance the livelihood in a sustainable way. The young researchers will have great opportunities to become research-active as well as to meet research experts from around the world, thereby getting into the right network, collaboration, knowledge sharing, keeping pace with the dynamic world, strengthening professional ties, challenge and growth.


The benefits of this conference are
• an ideal forum for communicating the research ideas related to technologies for sustainable development to the related field experts.
• allows the participants to stay up to date with developments in the proposed theme areas.
• motivation in the current research.
• Improve the presentation skills

As the theme says, this conference aims to stimulate sustainability and technological revolutions to make the planet greener and safer.
