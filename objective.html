THE OBEJCTIVE OF THE CONFERENCE

The conference focuses on bringing together experts from industries and academic institutions at national and international levels to the forum to discuss and to share their knowledge in developing a sustainable future so as to

• Conserve nature and energy thereby ensuring a better life for present and future generations.
• Lower the impact on the environment by reducing air, water, and soil pollution.
• Help in achieving long-term economic growth
